IEEE NITW Section Student Branch

We are NIT Warangal's Student Branch of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) - the world's largest technical organization for technological advancement.

Mission

Our mission is to help all those passionate about engineering broaden their understanding of the same.
The goals of our student branch include, but aren’t limited to:
1. To expand students’ horizon in engineering and technology, through the societies affiliated with our student branch when necessary, in hosting:

  a. Professors of our Institute
  b. Notable alumni of our Institute
  c. Nationally and internationally acclaimed experts.

For seminars and workshops to disseminate their expertise.

2. To provide opportunities for professional development through IEEE’s repertoire of services and applications.

3. To be a trusted source of educational services to support life-long learning, and pursue standards relevant to their practical application.
